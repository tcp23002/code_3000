# Ethics Statement

I think building AI comes with real responsibility because it can directly affect people’s lives. These systems aren’t neutral but rather they reflect the data and choices behind them, which means bias can easily show up if we're not careful.

For example, if an AI system is used in hiring but is trained on biased historical data, it could unfairly favor certain groups over others without being obvious. Situations like that show why transparency and regular evaluation are important, and why humans still need to stay involved in the decision-making process.

I believe developers should think beyond just whether something works and also consider who it impacts and how. Efficiency and automation are valuable, but not if they come at the cost of fairness or accountability. Overall, ethical technology should aim to reduce harm, promote fairness, and build trust with the people who rely on it.
